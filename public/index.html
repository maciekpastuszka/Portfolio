About me

Hi, I'm Front-end developer. The most important for me are quality of code, communication and learning new things. I value experience exchange that's why I like to work in team and often I take part in local IT events

I'am connected with IT industry since 2009 when at the first year of study I worked as a graphic designer. Since that time I had been searching my path, I tried different specialization from as I mentioned designer through web developer to CIO at startup. Additionally while studying I attended a students organization in which I was Vice prezident marketing and IT knowledge I translated into marketing activities.

Above experience, both as a regular employee and also freelancer drove me to the front-end developer path, where I have been focusing for almost two years.
